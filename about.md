Just in case curiosity has gotten the better of you, or you've reached the end of the internet, here's a little bit about me.

I’ve worn a lot of hats over the years—academic, architect, strategist, consultant. At the core of it all, I like solving complex problems, whether it’s helping businesses modernize their technology, figuring out how to automate my own workflows, or making sense of stuff  just for fun - and writing it all down in case I forget.

A Quick Snapshot:

Academic Roots: Started in glaciology and remote sensing, working with satellite data before it was cool or remotely affordable to do so.
Tech Career: Built Linux clusters, overhauled IT in higher ed, and now lead enterprise technology strategy in industry.
Cloud & AI: Deep in the world of Microsoft Azure, Linux, enterprise architecture, and AI adoption for mid-sized businesses.
Side Interests: Design, productivity systems, and figuring out what actually makes stuff work for the better.
Health & Fitness: Learning to swim properly, lifting things up and putting them down, and optimizing it all with data.

This space is where I collect thoughts, ideas, and things I find interesting. Expect a mix of enterprise IT, AI, automation, design, and the occasional deep dive into productivity and personal projects.

If that sounds useful—or at least entertaining—stick around.
